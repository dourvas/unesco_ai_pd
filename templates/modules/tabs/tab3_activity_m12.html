What strong auditors typically find
C1

Blanket prohibition — fails Elements 1, 2, and 3. Vague ("AI tools"), no contextual differentiation, no disclosure procedure.

C2

Teacher use clause — partial at best. No transparency requirement, no disclosure to students.

C3

Detection as conclusive — directly violates Element 6 (due process). Module 12 is explicit: no detection score alone is sufficient.

C4

Special needs — missing entirely. "Speak to your teacher" is not a policy provision.

C5

Data clause — missing GDPR compliance review and tool approval process.

C6

"As needed" review — fails Element 7. No scheduled date, no process, not a living document.
