Evidence-informed content

This module draws on professional development programmes delivered at the International Hellenic University, where participant evaluations were consistently positive. Content is further grounded in peer-reviewed research on AI in education.

▶ ▼ Why is this module part of your learning path?

The content in each module was selected through a three-step process: alignment with UNESCO AI competency indicators, grounding in current research on digital pedagogy, and informed by prior professional development programmes at IHU where similar content was positively evaluated by participants.

This module addresses {{ module.get_unesco_aspect_display }} at the {{ module.get_proficiency_level_display }} level — meaning the focus is on {% if module.proficiency_level == 'acquire' %} building foundational awareness and understanding. {% elif module.proficiency_level == 'deepen' %} applying and deepening knowledge through practice. {% else %} creating, leading, and sharing expertise with others. {% endif %}

Content selection rationale is documented in the PROODOS Research Framework (IHU Doctoral Research Programme).
